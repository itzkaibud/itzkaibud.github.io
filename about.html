About Me

Hi, I’m Kai Turley, a passionate sound designer for games and composer. With over 20 years of musical experience and a decade as an audio engineer, I specialize in creating immersive audio for games. After a recent shift to gaming, I’ve honed my skills through game jams and various projects over the past year.

I hold a BA in Music Composition from San Diego State University and an AA in Recording Production from MiraCosta Community College. Proficient in programming synthesizers, I craft unique soundscapes that enhance gameplay. Let’s create something extraordinary together!.
